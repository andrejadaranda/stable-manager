01 — Naming

Twelve candidates. One recommendation.

The brief: 1–2 syllables, EN-first, pronounceable in LT/PL/DE, equestrian-coded but not literal, ownable as a domain and trademark, built to carry a category for ten years.

01
Hoofbeat.
The system that keeps the rhythm of the yard. Two syllables, four consonants of attack, owns a clear sonic identity. Names the product's actual job — operational rhythm — instead of a piece of equipment. Equine Genie sells records. Stablebuzz sells noise. Hoofbeat sells the heartbeat of a working yard.

The five-test checklist (run before commit)

Domain · Trademark (WIPO + EUIPO + LT-VPB, classes 9/35/42) · Phone test with 5 stable owners · Search test (top 3 results) · Five-year fit at 1,000 stables.

02 — Positioning

Stop running your stable on WhatsApp.

Positioning statement

For European riding-school and livery-yard owners running 15–40 horses out of WhatsApp groups and Excel sheets, Hoofbeat is the stable management system that protects your horses while it runs your business — the only tool that puts horse workload, the schedule, and the invoice on the same screen. Unlike Equine Genie and Stablebuzz, we are built in Europe for European stables: SEPA, GDPR, and your local language from day one.

Manifesto (homepage About panel)

Stables don't fail because the people running them stopped caring. They fail because the day eats the work. The lesson runs late. The invoice doesn't get sent. The young horse goes out one ride too many. We built Hoofbeat for the trainer who would rather be in the arena than on a spreadsheet — and for the owner who refuses to choose between running a business and running a yard that horses thrive in.

Five brand pillars

01
Welfare first
Horse workload is a first-class object, not a checkbox. The dashboard shows tired horses in red.
02
Built by a stable, for stables
Domain credibility. Founder's yard is customer zero. Every release note signed.
03
European-native
LT, PL, DE, EN at launch. SEPA, Stripe Tax, GDPR export and erase live in MVP.
04
Simple over complete
30 minutes to value, not 4 hours of data entry. CSV import. Invite link.
05
Phone-first for trainers
The desktop is the back office; the phone is the workplace. Mobile PWA, one-tap check-in.
