### 2. What I ask of you

2.1 **Use the product weekly.** I built this for working stables. If you log fewer than 5 lessons in any given month after month 1, I will call you to find out what's wrong and fix it.

2.2 **Two referrals in your first 6 months.** You commit to introducing **2 stables in [your country]** to me — by warm email or WhatsApp intro. I take it from there. The standard you apply: stables you'd recommend to a friend, not a list of acquaintances.

2.3 **Honest feedback.** When something works, tell me. When something fails you, tell me with the specific. "It's slow" is useless; "the calendar week view takes 4 seconds to load on my phone" I can fix.

2.4 **One case study at month 6.** A 30-minute interview, transcribed, edited, published on `longrein.eu` with your stable name and your photo. You approve the final wording before publication. You can decline this clause and stay in the program — but the price stays the same; this isn't a discount-for-marketing trade.

2.5 **At month 12, give a clear yes or no.** Whatever you decide, tell me directly. I'd rather hear "no, here's why" than have you go quiet.

### 3. What I promise

3.1 **Your data is yours, legally and physically.** Stored in Supabase EU-Ireland. No third-party trackers, no advertising cookies, no resale of any kind. Privacy Policy: `longrein.eu/legal/privacy`.

3.2 **I will not surprise you.** Pricing, terms, and onboarding cadence are locked by this document. If I change something significant, you get 30 days written notice and the option to cancel and export.

3.3 **If Longrein closes** — for any reason — you get 90 days notice, an export of all your data in CSV format, and a refund of any paid period (not applicable during the Free Period).

3.4 **No lock-in tactics.** No auto-renewal traps. No "downgrade only by phone." No held-hostage data. You can leave at any time, before or after the Free Period.

## Variations / clauses you might need

### If they push back on the case study clause (2.4)

You don't lose the deal over this. Replace 2.4 with:

> 2.4 **Quarterly anonymous data point.** I share aggregated, anonymous metrics (e.g., "Founding member stables average X lessons per week") in public marketing. Your individual data is never identified.

This loses the testimonial but keeps the relationship. Don't accept "no public mention of any kind" — that severs the marketing benefit of founding members entirely.

### If they want their lawyer to review

Reply: "Of course — I'd want mine to do the same. Anything specific they push back on, send me; I'll respond within 48h."

This is a green flag. Stables with lawyers tend to be the well-run ones. Add 7 days to your timeline.

### If they ask to extend free period beyond 12 months

Don't. The boundary is what makes the offer credible. Reply:

> The 12 months free is calibrated against the 2 referrals + 1 case study. Pushing it longer changes the math on both sides. I'd rather lock it as-is now and revisit pricing transparently at month 12 if circumstances need it.

### If they ask for white-label / co-branding

Defer. Reply:

> Not in scope for the founding-member program — full-feature parity for all 10 founding stables is a non-negotiable. White-label or partnership options come on the table at month 12+, paid tier, and we can talk then if your stable would still be interested.
