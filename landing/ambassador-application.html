Ambassador FAQ

What counts as a referral?
A referral only counts when a person you referred becomes a paying Longrein customer using your referral code. Signups, registrations, free accounts and inactive users do not count toward your tier or your commissions.
When are commissions earned?
Commission is earned only on completed, paid purchases made with your code. The amount depends on your current tier at the time of the purchase: €5 Bronze, €7 Silver, €10 Gold, €15 Platinum.
How do tier upgrades work?
Upgrades are automatic as your number of successful paid referrals grows — Bronze 0–4, Silver 5–14, Gold 15–39, Platinum 40+. Each tier unlocks a higher commission and additional benefits.
How do discounts work?
Anyone who buys Longrein with an ambassador's referral code pays €40 instead of €50 — a 20% discount. You earn your tier's commission for each completed paid purchase made with your code.
Is the ambassador role paid?
The role itself is unpaid. Selected ambassadors receive free Longrein access, a personal discount code, and commission opportunities based on approved paid referrals.
