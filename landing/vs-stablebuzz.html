Same category, different design philosophy

Both Longrein and StableBuzz are stable management platforms — schedule lessons, manage clients, process payments, track horses. The difference is in how each is built and who each is built for. StableBuzz has a long history serving primarily English-speaking and North American customers. Longrein is purpose-built for the European market in 2026, with EU data residency, native SEPA and local payment methods, GDPR-first architecture, and a mobile-first interface that works on the phone before the desktop.

This page lays out the differences honestly so you can pick the tool that fits your stable best. If StableBuzz is the better fit for you, use StableBuzz. We are not trying to win every customer — we are trying to win European riding schools and livery yards who want a modern, EU-built alternative.

Why EU stables specifically pick Longrein

  • Your client data never leaves the EU. Frankfurt-hosted Postgres, no cross-Atlantic transfers, no Schrems II headaches.
  • Stripe handles SEPA Direct Debit, iDEAL, Bancontact, and card payments out of the box. Clients pay how they expect to pay.
  • VAT-ready invoicing for stables that need it.
  • Interface translates cleanly to the European context — €, 24-hour time, ISO dates, metric units.
  • Support and product team based in Lithuania, working in your time zone.

Where StableBuzz might still be the right pick

StableBuzz has been around longer and has a deeper bench of niche features that have accumulated over years — competition tracking, breeding records, more sophisticated boarding configurations for very large operations. If your stable has a complex breeding programme or runs a competition circuit, StableBuzz's depth in those areas may matter to you.

If your priority is operational software for an EU riding school or livery yard with 10 to 60 horses, Longrein is the right shape for the job.

Frequently asked

Can I migrate from StableBuzz to Longrein?
Yes. Export horses, clients, and lessons from StableBuzz as CSV, then import via Longrein Settings → Import. Most stables are operational in under an hour. For founding members we do the migration for you.
What about my historical payment records?
Stripe payment history stays in Stripe — independent of which app you use as a frontend. CSV export of past invoices brings the records into Longrein. No data loss.
Will Longrein add the features StableBuzz has that you don't?
We ship weekly. Most of the gap is intentional — we are deliberately not bloating the platform with niche features. If something matters to enough European stables, it gets shipped fast. Roadmap is public on request.
