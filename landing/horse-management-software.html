Welfare data usually lives nowhere

Ask most stables where the vaccination dates are and the answer is a wallet calendar, a WhatsApp message, or the vet's memory. When an inspector, an insurer or a new owner asks for the record, it has to be reconstructed from scratch. And the quiet welfare risk — one or two horses doing most of the work while others stand idle — is invisible until a horse breaks down.

Longrein turns each horse profile into the welfare record, and connects it to the work the horse actually does.

What it tracks

Health records

Vaccinations, vet visits, farrier and dental dates, medication and a full activity history — one timeline per horse.

Reminders

Automatic reminders for upcoming vaccinations, farrier and vet appointments so nothing is missed.

Weight & condition

Log weight and condition over time and see the trend, not just the latest number.

Workload tracking

Hours worked per horse per week, with flags for horses overworked or idle more than 14 days. Keep the rotation fair.

Weather alerts

Frost and heat alerts against your stable's blanket and turnout thresholds, emailed before the cold or heat hits.

Guest contributor links

A groom, vet or farrier logs an event via a private link — no Longrein account needed. It lands on the horse's record instantly.
