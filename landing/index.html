Common questions

Asked & answered.

Who is Longrein for?
European riding schools, livery yards, and equestrian businesses managing roughly 10 to 60 horses. Also: solo horse owners on the Personal plan who just want to log their own riding and care.
Is my data really safe?
Yes. We're EU-hosted (Frankfurt) and GDPR-compliant from day one. Stripe handles all card data, so we never see it. Every stable's data is mathematically isolated from every other stable's data via Row-Level Security — not just policy. One click to export everything as CSV. One click to delete everything.
Does it work on my phone?
Yes. Longrein is mobile-first. Trainers check in lessons from the arena, clients pay invoices on their phone, owners run the calendar from anywhere. A native iOS app ships in summer 2026 — the web app is fully responsive in the meantime.
Can I try it before I pay?
Yes. Stable plan: 14-day free trial. Personal plan: 7-day free trial. Card required at signup (we keep trial farmers out), but never charged before the trial ends. Cancel any time in one click and you're never billed.
What about my existing data — horses, clients, lessons?
Import from CSV in Settings → Import. Most stables are fully operational in under an hour. If you need help, write to hello@longrein.eu and we'll walk you through it.
Do my clients need to pay for an account?
No. Clients (riders) and horse owners use Longrein for free. You pay one flat €49/month and invite unlimited clients and horse owners under your stable.
What if I'm just a single-horse owner without a stable?
The Personal plan is built for you. €9/month for up to 2 horses, €15/month for up to 5. Log rides, vet visits, expenses, and goals. No business features cluttering the UI.
Can I really get €25/month for life?
If you're one of the first fifteen stables — yes. Write to hello@longrein.eu with your stable name, size, and a short note. Hands-on onboarding, 3 months free + €299 one-time onboarding fee, then €25/month locked forever, even when our public rate goes up.
