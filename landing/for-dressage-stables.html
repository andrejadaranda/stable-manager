What dressage stables run on Longrein

A dressage stable is operationally different from a riding school. The lessons are private or semi-private. The clients are committed long-term. The horses are higher-value, often owned by others. Welfare and conditioning matter more than headcount. Longrein is shaped for exactly that profile.

Recurring private lessons

Weekly slots per rider per horse. Set once, repeats. Drag-and-drop for one-off changes. Holiday and clinic calendar suppresses dates.

Per-horse identity

Bloodline (sire, dam), microchip, passport, FEI ID, discipline, height in cm, colour, sex — all on one Identification card.

Training session log

Each session logged against the horse. Optional GPS track for hack sessions. Notes private to staff or visible to owner.

Welfare per horse

Weight log, vet visits, farrier visits, vaccinations, idle alerts. The record that proves you took care of the horse.

Owner portal

Each owner sees their horse's schedule, welfare, balance, and invoices. Pays monthly boarding via Stripe automatically.

Multi-rider scheduling

Multiple riders on multiple horses, no double-booking. Working hours and arena capacity enforced.

Stripe billing

SEPA, card, or local methods. Packages of lessons, monthly boarding, one-off invoices. No spreadsheet.

Public stable page

Branded longrein.eu/s/your-stable with your sport horses' bios — useful for prospective clients and breeding enquiries.

Mobile-first

Check the schedule from the arena. Log a session from your phone. PWA installs to home screen; native iOS app summer 2026.

What Longrein is not

Honest framing: Longrein is not a competition entry platform, not a scoresheet tracker, not a breeding pedigree database. If your stable's primary need is national-level competition management or pedigree research, you'll want federation tools alongside Longrein. The two complement each other — Longrein handles the daily operation, federation tools handle the competition surface.

Frequently asked

Can I record training notes that the owner does not see?
Yes. Each horse has an internal Notes (private) field visible only to staff. Public Bio is visible to the owner and on the public stable page.
Does Longrein handle FEI passports?
FEI ID is a field on the horse Identification card and is filterable. We don't fetch from the FEI database; you enter it once.
What about the morning groom workflow?
Each groom or stable hand gets a Trainer or Employee account with their own dashboard. Vet and farrier can post into a horse's health log via a guest-contributor magic link without needing an account.
