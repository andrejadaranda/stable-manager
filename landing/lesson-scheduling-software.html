Scheduling lessons the hard way

A riding school runs 80 to 200 lessons a week — half recurring weekly slots, half one-off bookings, spread across three or four instructors and a dozen horses. Keep that in a paper diary or a shared spreadsheet and something always slips: a horse double-booked across two lessons, a rider who swears they have three lessons left, a Saturday group nobody confirmed. Generic calendar apps don't know that a horse can only work so many hours, so they can't protect you from it.

Longrein replaces the diary with a scheduling system that understands lessons, horses and packages together.

What you get

Recurring slots

Set a weekly lesson once; instances generate automatically. Drag and drop to move a lesson. Holiday calendar suppresses closed dates.

Instructor calendars

Each trainer has their own calendar with their lessons, horses and notes. The owner sees the union of all calendars; trainers see their slice.

Online booking

Riders and parents book or request lessons from your public stable page. Requests land in your inbox for one-tap approval.

Package balances

Each completed lesson decrements the rider's package automatically. No more arguments about how many lessons remain.

Horse workload

See how many hours each horse works per week. Longrein flags horses that are overworked or idle so the rotation stays fair.

Reminders

Automatic confirmations and reminders keep no-shows down and keep parents off your phone at 22:00 on a Sunday.

Part of the whole platform

Scheduling is one surface of Longrein's stable management software. The same lessons feed billing, horse welfare and your finance dashboard — so a completed lesson updates the rider's balance and your revenue at the same time. See it in context for riding schools.

Frequently asked

Can I set recurring weekly lessons?
Yes. Set a weekly slot once and instances auto-create. Drag and drop to reschedule; a holiday calendar suppresses closed dates.
Can clients book lessons themselves?
Yes. Your public stable page lets riders and parents request or book online. Requests land in your inbox for one-tap approval, with an automatic confirmation email.
Does it handle lesson packages?
Yes. Sell packages of any size; each completed booking decrements the rider's balance automatically.
Can I use it on my phone?
Yes. Mark lessons complete from the arena; the whole platform is mobile-first. Native iOS app summer 2026.
