# Founding Members offer · Longrein

**1-page offer template — share by email or in person. Brand voice: direct, not chatty. EN-first; Andreja personally translates LT/PL where needed.**

---

## The offer

We're inviting **10 European riding schools and livery yards** to be the first stables on Longrein.

You get **12 months free** of the full product — calendar, lesson billing, horse welfare dashboard, client portal, packages, boarding charges, audit log, the lot. No credit card. No trial cliff.

In exchange we ask for three things:

1. **One 30-minute call per month** for 12 months — what's working, what's broken, what's missing.
2. **A short case study** with your name, your stable's name, and a photo when we publish in 2027. We write the words; you approve them.
3. **Two referrals** to other stables in your country in the first 6 months — one warm intro by email is enough.

That's it. No upsell at month 13 — we keep your stable on the same plan you'd qualify for at our public pricing, but we honour the founding-member rate (capped at the lower of €30/month or whatever 50% of our then-current public price works out to, for the lifetime of your account). The contract is one-page; you can leave any time.

## Why it matters to us

You give us what no founder can buy: real reps from a real yard. Every welfare alert that misfires, every clumsy mobile flow, every gap between what Longrein does and what you actually need at 5am in the arena — we want to hear it. That feedback writes the product roadmap for the next 100 customers.

## Why it matters to you

- **You shape the tool.** The 30-minute calls aren't ceremonial — they go straight into Sav. 1 of every product cycle.
- **You don't pay for it during the part of the year where you might still be deciding.** A normal trial ends in 14 days. Twelve months tells us we're serious about earning your stable.
- **You're founding member #1–10 forever.** That doesn't mean a louder email signature. It means the support thread you opened in May 2026 is still answered by Andreja directly when you ask in 2030.

## What you actually get during the 12 months

- Calendar with drag-to-reschedule, week + day + print views
- Per-horse profiles with welfare workload tracking (the real wedge — every other tool buries this)
- Lesson packages, boarding charges, per-client misc charges
- Client portal so riders see their own lessons + balances
- Audit log — who did what, when (compliance-grade)
- Phone-first throughout — your trainers can run a session log from the arena
- Direct email + WhatsApp line to Andreja for anything that breaks
- Welcome-to-Longrein onboarding call (45 min, EN or LT)

What you don't get during this run: SMS reminders (Q3'26), Stripe self-pay (Q4'26), automated client self-booking (Q3'26), DE/PL UI translation (Q3'26 for PL, Q4'26 for DE — we onboard EU yards in EN for now and Andreja personally helps in LT).

## How to say yes

Reply to the outreach email with:
- Your stable's name, country, and roughly how many horses + active clients
- Best date for a 45-min onboarding call (we ship a Cal.com link)

We onboard one stable per business day during May–June 2026. First 10 to confirm get the slots; we'll keep a waitlist and run a second round in autumn 2026 if a founding member doesn't engage past month 2.
