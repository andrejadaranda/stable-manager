# Longrein — Personal Sustainability & Burnout Prevention Plan

**Klausimas:** Kaip solo bootstrap founder'ė, su primary income on the line, su 24-mėn. commitment'u, valdo asmeninę bandwidth taip, kad pasiekia M24 with both Longrein AND her health intact?
**Recommendation:** Strukturizuota weekly rhythm + explicit decision triggers + community + mandatory recovery — NE „self-care" theatre.
**Critical insight:** „Burnout" yra ne charakter weakness'o ženklas. Tai PREDICTABLE FAILURE MODE solo founder'iui be discipline'os. Šitas dokumentas yra apsaugos mechanizmas, ne motivational poster'is.

Šitas dokumentas yra explicit intervention BEFORE crisis, NE after.

## Dalis 1 — Kas yra burnout (technical definition, ne pop-psychology)

Burnout yra physical + emotional + cognitive exhaustion state, atsirandantis kai effort > recovery 6+ savaičių iš eilės.

3 dimensijos (per Maslach Burnout Inventory — clinical standard):

### 1. Emotional exhaustion
- Symptom: „tired even after 8h sleep"
- Symptom: „can't be enthusiastic about anything"
- Symptom: „weekend feels like work"
- Severity: yellow at 4 weeks, red at 8 weeks

### 2. Depersonalization / cynicism
- Symptom: „customers are just numbers now"
- Symptom: „I don't care if this works"
- Symptom: „what's the point"
- Severity: yellow at 6 weeks, red at 10 weeks (often appears LATE)

### 3. Reduced personal accomplishment
- Symptom: „I'm not as good at this as I thought"
- Symptom: „other founders make it look easy"
- Symptom: „my customers would be better off without me"
- Severity: most dangerous — leads to quitting decisions

**Critical:** if you check 2+ symptoms across 2+ dimensions for 4+ consecutive weeks → MEDICAL DEFINITION of burnout. NOT optional to ignore.

## Dalis 4 — Boundary discipline (concrete rules)

Rules that LOOK like work-from-home advice but are actually critical:

### Phone

- WhatsApp: ALL Longrein customer chats muted from 19:00-08:00 + weekends
- Email: notifications OFF on phone (only check via desktop, structured times)
- Stripe alerts: enable critical only (failed payments)
- Calendar: block „personal time" → no „can you talk now" requests

### Physical workspace

- Longrein desktop work in dedicated location (NOT bedroom, NOT couch)
- TJK ops work in arena/office (already separate)
- „End of day" ritual: close laptop, leave dedicated workspace = work is over
- Phone NOT in bedroom (charge in kitchen or living room)

### Communication boundaries with customers

Auto-responder during off-hours:
```
Thank you for your email. I'm currently offline but will respond on the next business day. For urgent product issues, please email support@longrein.eu (typically faster response). For non-urgent topics, I'll get back within 24 hours.

Andreja
```

This sets EXPECTATION explicitly. „Not always available" is not „bad service."

### Boundary with self

The hardest one.

- „One more email before bed" → NO
- „I'll just check Stripe metrics" → NO at 23:00
- „Founder's never off" → FALSE

Founders who survive 24 mėn. are the ones who treat boundary discipline as PRODUCT QUALITY for themselves.

## Dalis 5 — Physical health protocols

Your body is the operational asset of this entire business. Treat it as critical infrastructure.

### Sleep

- Target: 7-8 hours/night
- Bedtime: 22:30-23:00 (forces 6:00 wake)
- Phone in kitchen at 21:30 (no scrolling in bed)
- Bedroom temp 18-19°C
- IF sleeping <6 hrs 2 nights in a row: skip morning workout, take 30 min walk only

**Rationale:** chronic sleep deprivation reduces cognitive performance by 30%+. Worse than working 4 hours less per week with full brain.

### Riding (yes, this counts as health)

- Continue riding 3-4×/week minimum
- ONE ride per week purely for joy, NOT instruction or testing
- This is your unique advantage: physical+emotional integration via horse contact

If riding becomes „chore" — burnout signal Tier 1.

### Food

- Eat 3 real meals/day at predictable times (12:00, 18:00, leftover lunch sometimes 09:00 if not breakfast person)
- Protein at every meal (strength baseline matters)
- Limit caffeine after 14:00
- Hydration: 2L water/day minimum

### Exercise (beyond riding)

- 2× per week 30-min cardio (walk, run, bike) — outside if weather allows
- 1× per week strength work (kettlebell, bodyweight, gym)
- Weekend long walk (90 min) — meditation effect

If you skip 2 weeks of exercise → Tier 2 burnout signal.

### Medical

- Annual physical (kraujo tyrimai, etc.) — schedule M6 + M18
- Dentist 2× per year
- Eye exam annually (screen time damages)

## Dalis 6 — Mental health protocols

Equally critical infrastructure. Don't skip.

### Therapy / coaching

Recommendation: monthly session with therapist OR executive coach.

- Therapist: helps with emotional regulation, anxiety, family/relationship dynamics
- Executive coach: helps with founder-specific isolation, decision-making, strategic clarity
- Cost: €60-€100/session in LT (€720-€1200/yr)
- THIS IS IN YOUR YEAR-1 BUDGET — don't cut it

When to start: M3 (when launch dust settles, founder loneliness sets in)
When essential: if 2+ burnout symptoms (Dalis 1) for 3+ weeks

### Meditation / reflection

NOT prescriptive about app or technique. But:
- 10 min/day quiet reflection (walk, journal, sit on TJK arena rail)
- Friday afternoon: 30 min „week in review" — what went well, what's stuck

### Reading / disconnection

- 30 min/day NON-WORK reading (fiction, history, poetry)
- Phone in kitchen during this time
- Identifies you as „person beyond Longrein founder"

### Hobby outside work

You DO need a hobby that has nothing to do with horses or software. Examples:
- Cooking (one new recipe / week)
- Music (instrument, choir, lessons)
- Language learning (DE, given DACH plans)
- Photography
- Volunteering (community garden, animal shelter)

If you can't name a hobby outside Longrein + TJK → start one this month. Identity beyond your work.

## Dalis 7 — Community needs (founder loneliness solution)

The single most predictive factor of founder burnout: ISOLATION.

You need 3 distinct community sources:

### 1. Founder peers (most important)

Other solo bootstrap founders, ideally in vertical SaaS, ideally in EU.

How to find:
- LT Tech Forum founder community
- Indie Hackers (online community, free)
- Bootstrapped Founder podcast community
- LinkedIn targeted connections (search „solo bootstrap" + „founder")

How to use:
- Monthly virtual coffee with 1 specific founder peer
- Quarterly in-person if local
- WhatsApp group with 3-5 peer founders for casual venting

**Why critical:** family/friends don't understand the specific texture of founder problems. Other founders do.

### 2. Local equestrian community

You already have this via TJK. Maintain it actively.

- Trainer relationships (1-on-1 conversations beyond work)
- Client friendships (selective, age-matched)
- Industry events (LT equestrian federation gatherings)

### 3. Personal — family + friends OUTSIDE founder world

Don't lose your pre-Longrein friends. They're your reality check.

- Weekly call with 1 close friend (not work talk — actually personal)
- Monthly family dinner (or whatever cadence fits family structure)
- Annual reunion-style catchup with old friends

**Critical:** founders who lose all non-founder friends report highest burnout rates. You become only your work, then you ARE only your work, then your work consumes you entirely.

## Dalis 8 — Recovery protocols (when you ARE burning)

You will hit yellow zones. Maybe red. Have protocols ready.

### Yellow zone protocol (4-week emotional exhaustion)

Step 1: Take 1 full day off (NO Longrein work, NO TJK work — find coverage)
Step 2: 7-day diet of basics — sleep 8+ hrs, eat well, exercise 30 min daily, NO alcohol
Step 3: Reduce work week to 50 hrs for next 14 days
Step 4: Re-evaluate after 21 days

Cost: ~21 days slower progress. Worth it vs spiral.

### Red zone protocol (sustained burnout signs)

Step 1: 7-day complete pause (off-grid if possible — no email, no calls, no metrics)
Step 2: Therapist + GP visit (rule out physical causes)
Step 3: Honest scope reduction conversation — what can be paused, delayed, dropped?
Step 4: Inform 3 trusted advisors / mentors / peer founders that you're recovering
Step 5: Return at 60% capacity for 30 days, scale back to 100% only if feeling well

Cost: ~6 weeks slower progress. SURVIVAL of the venture.

### Crisis protocol (panic attacks, suicidal ideation, can't function)

Step 1: STOP. Inform partner / closest support person.
Step 2: GP visit within 48 hours, OR mental health hotline if acute
Step 3: Inform Longrein customers via email — you'll be offline 14-30 days for medical reasons
Step 4: Inform TJK trainers — they manage in your absence
Step 5: Take medical leave. Return only when professionals advise.

Cost: 30-90 days. Business may suffer (FMs may churn). But YOU survive. That's the only metric that ultimately matters.

## Dalis 9 — Decision triggers (objective markers)

Per Year-1 financial model: „If founder works >55 hrs/week 3 weeks consecutive — burnout risk." Operationalize this:

### Weekly self-check (Friday afternoon, 5 min)

5 questions, 1-5 scale (1=terrible, 5=great):

1. Sleep quality this week
2. Energy level this week
3. Mood / emotional state
4. Physical health (any new pains, fatigue?)
5. Hours worked this week

**Score interpretation:**
- 20-25: GREEN. All systems healthy.
- 15-19: YELLOW. Watch for next week's score.
- 10-14: ORANGE. Consider intervention (per Dalis 8).
- 5-9: RED. Mandatory protocol activation.

Track in same Notion CRM page each week. Look for TRENDS, not single readings.

### Monthly decision review (1st of each month)

Self-questions:
1. Am I excited about Longrein this month? (Y/N)
2. Did I take Sundays off? (4 of 4? 3? 2? 1? 0?)
3. Did I exercise 3+ times/week? (4 weeks)
4. Last social event with friend (NOT work-related)?
5. Was there a week I worked 60+ hours?

If 3+ negative answers → schedule extra recovery week before next month.

### Quarterly review with trusted advisor

Talk through your state with 1 trusted person every 3 mo:
- Therapist if you have one
- Peer founder
- Family member who knows you well

They see things you miss. Listen.

## Dalis 10 — Annual rest planning (mandatory)

Founder time off is NOT a benefit. It is operational maintenance.

### Year 1 mandatory rest plan

- **2 weeks summer vacation** (somewhere REMOTE — TJK covers via trainers)
  - Schedule: July 14-28, 2026 (between launch and FM ramp-up)
  - Plan: completely off-grid IF possible (no emails, automatic responder, customer expectations set)

- **1 week winter break** (around Christmas)
  - Schedule: Dec 22, 2026 - Jan 2, 2027
  - Plan: family + recovery, no Longrein work

- **3-day breaks quarterly** (4× per year)
  - Schedule: end of each quarter
  - Plan: extend regular weekend by 1 day, do something rejuvenating

**Total Year 1 time off: 2 + 1 + (4 × 3) = 27 days**

This is LESS than typical EU employee (30+ days). It's the MINIMUM founder needs.

### Coverage strategy

Before each break:
- 2 weeks before: announce break to FMs („I'll be off DATE-DATE")
- 1 week before: WhatsApp + email auto-responders set
- Day before: TJK trainers briefed on emergency contact
- During: emergency-only contact (you DECIDE what's emergency, NOT customer)

After each break:
- Day 1 back: catch up on critical items only
- Day 2: full schedule resume

## Dalis 13 — One last thing

Most founder burnout advice is bullshit. „Wake up at 5 AM," „cold showers," „crush it," „no excuses." That's performance theatre, not sustainability.

Real sustainability is boring:
- Sleep enough
- Eat real food
- Move your body
- See other humans
- Have boundaries
- Take time off
- Talk to someone when you're struggling

You already know all of this.

The question is: will you ACT on it when the pressure to skip it is real?

The founders who survive 24 mėn. solo bootstrap are not the ones with the most willpower. They are the ones who built systems that PROTECTED them from their own willpower-based decisions to overwork.

This document is one such system. Print it. Reread it monthly. Update it as your situation evolves.

The goal is not to build Longrein at the expense of your health.

The goal is to build Longrein WITH your health intact.

If those two things are in conflict — your health wins. Always. Because Longrein without you = no Longrein.
