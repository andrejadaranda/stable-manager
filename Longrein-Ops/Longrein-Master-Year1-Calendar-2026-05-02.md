## Dalis 8 — How to use this calendar

### Setup (one-time, ~30 min)

1. Open longrein.team@gmail.com Google Calendar
2. Create calendar „Longrein Year 1"
3. Manually enter all critical dates from Dalis 6 as events
4. Set reminders: 1 day before + 1 week before for major events
5. Add recurring events from Dalis 2-3:
   - Daily 6:00 TJK ops
   - Wed 17:00 protected riding
   - Sat 14:00 weekend off
   - Friday 16:00 weekly self-check
   - Monthly: first Pirmadienis 09:00 monthly review
   - Quarterly: competitive review + 3-day breaks

### Monthly maintenance (~5 min, 1st of each month)

1. Review prior month: hit dates? missed any? why?
2. Verify next month dates still accurate
3. Adjust if business reality has shifted

### Quarterly maintenance (~30 min, end of Q)

1. Review entire upcoming quarter
2. Cross-check with financial model + sustainability plan
3. Adjust based on real performance vs LIKELY case
4. Update this document if dates shift significantly

### Annual maintenance (~3 hrs, end of year)

1. Full Year-N review against this calendar
2. Year-N+1 calendar generation (similar structure, updated dates)
3. Lessons learned document (what worked, what didn't)

## Dalis 9 — Common calendar mistakes (NEdaryti)

1. **Setting dates without reminders** — out of sight, out of mind
2. **Over-scheduling deep work** — 3-hr blocks max, brain can't sustain longer
3. **No buffer between meetings** — back-to-back FM calls = exhaustion
4. **Skipping mandatory rest** — „I'll take vacation later" = burnout in 18 mo
5. **Not tracking actual vs planned** — without retrospective, no learning
6. **Missing decision gate dates** — late decisions = late actions = late results
7. **No personal events scheduled** — work fills void, family/friends suffer
8. **Yearly calendar that ignores monthly reality** — annual plan must adapt monthly
