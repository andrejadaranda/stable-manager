## Dalis 4 — Activity setup (kad LinkedIn algoritmas tau dirbtų)

### Connection strategy

Pirmų 4 sav. — actively grow connections (NE wait passively):

**Daily routine:** 5 connection requests, KIEKVIENAS su asmeniniu žinute (~30 chars max). Pavyzdys:

```
Hi [Vardas], saw your post about [konkretus dalykas]. I'm building Longrein — European stable management software. Would love to connect with someone in [their specialty/industry].
```

**Targets pirmų 30 dienų (priority order):**
1. EU equestrian industry contacts (yard owners, federation reps, vet clinics)
2. EU SaaS founders (peer network — they amplify content)
3. EU B2B journalists (Sifted, EU-Startups, Heureka, Wired, plus LT/PL equivalents)
4. EU early-stage investors who write about vertical SaaS (NOT for fundraise — for ecosystem visibility)
5. People who comment on YOUR posts (auto-warm leads)

**Target connection count:**
- Pre-launch: 200
- M1: 500
- M3: 1,000
- M6: 2,000
- M12: 5,000

5,000 connections + 5,000 followers (separate metric) = serious LinkedIn presence per founder per industry.

### Engagement habits

**Daily 30 min routine** (per LinkedIn First 30 Days plan):
- 5 thoughtful comments on relevant posts (3+ sentences each)
- 5 personalized connection requests
- Reply to all comments on YOUR posts within 4 hours
- React to 10–15 posts (not random — strategic — boost people you want to be associated with)

### What to NEVER do on LinkedIn

- Generic „congratulations on your new role" comments
- 🚀 emojis or other corporate cringe
- „I'm so excited to announce..." headline framing
- Tagging unrelated influencers hoping for amplification
- Auto-DM new connections with sales pitches
- Vote farming (random polls just for engagement)
- Republishing same post on multiple platforms simultaneously (LinkedIn deprioritizes cross-posted content)
