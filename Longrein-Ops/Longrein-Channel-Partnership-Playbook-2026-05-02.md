# Longrein — Channel Partnership Playbook

**Tikslas:** Pridėti 2–3x velocity multiplier'ą virš direct sales. Solo founder'is be channel'o cap'ina apie 150 customers per 36 mėn. Su 2 stipriais partnerships'ais — pasiekiamas 400+ customer'ių pool.
**ARR impact estimate:** +€280K–€520K incremental ARR per Year 3 (priklausomai nuo partner deal'o struct'ūros).
**Kritinis insight:** vertical-niche SaaS'e (500–2,000 stables EU) channel'as nėra „nice to have" — tai yra vienintelis kelias pasiekti SOM saturacijos.

---

## TL;DR

5 partner tipai, ranged pagal expected ROI:

1. **National equestrian federations (LSF, PZJ)** — highest leverage. 1 partner = access į 300+ qualified yards. Negotiation timeline 3–6 mėn., kaina €0–€2,000/yr per federation.
2. **Top vet clinics (regional, 3–5 in LT/PL)** — high trust signal. 1 partner = 50–100 yard introductions over 12 mėn.
3. **Feed/supplement suppliers (LT/PL national distributors)** — high frequency touchpoint. Partnership via co-marketing, ne direct referral.
4. **Accountants serving equestrian SMBs** — niche but sticky. 1 accountant = 5–15 stables.
5. **Equestrian magazines/influencers** — lowest leverage but easiest to start. Sponsored content, ~5–15 customer attribution per campaign.

NEdaryti šių klaidų:
- NEsipridėk visus 5 vienu metu — pirmas du (federation + 1 vet) per pirmus 6 mėn.
- NEsutik su revenue share'iu prieš įsitvirtinant pricing'ą (pirmi 30 customers full price prieš channel discount'ą)
- NEdėk channel'o priorit'u prieš product-market fit (turi 5+ delighted Founding Members'ų prieš channel pitch'ą)

## Dalis 1 — Partnership timing strategy

Mūsų scenario: solo founder, post-Founding-Members launch, target 30 paying customers M12.

### M0–M6 (May–November 2026): NO channel work

Reason: prieš channel partnership'ą tu turi:
- 5+ paying customers (proof, ne just FM)
- 1 case study with quantified ROI
- Stable product (no major bugs that would embarrass partner)
- Public landing su pricing'u

Fokuoti šitam laikotarpiui — direct outreach + content. Channel'as starts at M6.

### M6–M12: Federation #1 + Vet partnership #1 (sequential, ne paralleliai)

**Sequential reason:** kiekvienam partnership'ui reikia ~30–50 val. founder'io laiko per 3 mėn. (negotiation, integration, soft launch). Negali daryti dviejų vienu metu solo.

- M6: kontaktuoti LT Federation (LSF) su pirmu pitch'u
- M9: tikslas — sutarties su LSF signed
- M9: paralleliai pradėti vet clinic outreach (jau turim leverage'ą iš federation tag'o)
- M12: tikslas — vet partnership #1 signed

### M12–M24: Add federation #2 (Polska Związek Jeździecki) + Vet #2 + Accountant #1

Once first 2 partnerships proven (KPIs hit per Dalis 7), repeat'ink modelį PL marketo.

### M24+: Add magazines, influencers, feed suppliers

Lowest leverage — leave for last when team has 1–2 part-time partner managers (NE solo founder).

## Dalis 2 — Partner Type #1: National Equestrian Federations

### Kontekstas

LT: **Lietuvos Sportininkų Federacija (LSF)** — apie 300+ aktyvių member yards.
PL: **Polski Związek Jeździecki (PZJ)** — apie 1,000+ aktyvių member yards.
DE: **Deutsche Reiterliche Vereinigung (FN)** — 8,000+ yards. Mūsų DACH expansion partner.

Šios organizacijos turi:
- Email list visiems member'iams (nemonetizuotas channel)
- Annual conferences / general meetings (audience — exact ICP)
- Member newsletter / magazine
- Education/training programs (pre-existing pipe'as content'ui)

### What we want from federation

1. Endorsement (federation rekomenduoja Longrein savo member'iams)
2. Email blast access (1–4 email'ai per metus)
3. Conference booth / speaking slot
4. Newsletter feature (1–2 per metus)
5. „Official partner" logo lock'as ant longrein.eu

### What federation wants (and what we offer)

Federations rarely want pure cash. Jie nori:
- **Member benefit** (perceived value to dues-paying members) — discount visiems members'iams (siūlom 10–15% off Longrein)
- **Education content** (turinys, kurį jie gali naudoti nemokamai) — co-branded webinarai apie welfare, operations, GDPR
- **Brand alignment** (asocijuoti su modernumu) — startup'ai prideda spalvą prie traditional federation'o
- **Small revenue share** (kartais — nepradžiai, bet kaip safety net) — 10% revenue share customer'iams, kuriuos jie attributuoja

### Partnership model siūlymas

```
LSF–Longrein Partnership Agreement (proposed structure)

Term: 12 mėn., auto-renew jei abi pusės sutinka

LSF gives:
- 4 email blasts/metai pristatant Longrein savo members'iams
- 1 conference booth (laisvai duotas) + 30-min speaking slot
- 2 newsletter features (250+ words each)
- "LSF Official Partner" badge usage rights
- Permission iškabinti LSF logo ant longrein.eu/partners

Longrein gives:
- 15% discount visiems LSF dues-paying members (perpetual, ne first-year only)
- 5% revenue share LSF iš LSF-attributed customer'ių pirmus 24 mėn.
- 2 free educational webinars per metai (co-branded, LSF members can access free)
- 5 "LSF Member Premium" upgrades per metai (free 6-mo upgrade for selected stables)
- Quarterly KPI report'as LSF (customers acquired, member testimonials)

Mutual:
- 12-mo evaluation point — abi pusės gali walk away
- Performance KPIs reviewed quarterly
```

### Estimated economics

- LSF members: ~300
- Penetration target Year 1: 5% → 15 customer'ių
- Penetration target Year 2: 12% → 36 customer'ių
- 5% revenue share to LSF: minimal cost (~€800/yr after Year 2)
- Customer Acquisition Cost (CAC) per LSF: ~€50 vs direct outreach CAC ~€200


### KPIs federation partnership'ui

- New customers acquired per LSF channel per quarter (target: 4 in Q1, 8 in Q2, 12 in Q3, 15 in Q4)

## Dalis 3 — Partner Type #2: Vet Clinics

### Kontekstas

Vet'ai yra unique — jie yra TRUSTED touchpoint, lankantys yard'us 1–4× per mėn. Kai vet'as sako „naudoju Longrein vakcinacijų tracking'ui — tu turėtum irgi pažiūrėti", stable owner'is klauso kaip nieko kito.

Top regional vet clinics LT (~5 didžiausios):
- Equine Vet Klinika (Vilnius)
- VetMedica (Kaunas)
- Žirgų sveikatos centras (Trakai)
- (kitos 2 — research time)

PL vet clinics — atskiras research, bet bendras dydis 20–30 didžiausių.

### What we want from vet clinics

1. **Referral**: kai vet'as lanko stable, paminėti Longrein vaccinacijų tracking'ą
2. **Health data integration**: vet sistemos rašo tiesiai į stable'es Longrein health records (saves vet'ui clicks, saves stable owner'iui transcribing)
3. **Co-marketing**: shared content apie horse welfare, preventive care
4. **Logo lock**: „Trusted by [Vet Clinic Name]" landing page'e

### What vet clinics want

Vet'ai nori:
- **Operational efficiency** — mažiau handoff'ų tarp vet ↔ stable
- **Better data quality** — pacijentai (žirgai) atvyksta su pilna istorija, ne „šiandien jam vakarcina galimai"
- **Marketing exposure** — vet clinic'os yra B2C-ish (klientai = horse owners), bet B2B'is for stables. Šaltinis naujų stables.
- **Revenue (kartais)** — small fee per referral, bet tipiškai ne primary motivation

### Partnership model siūlymas

```
Vet Clinic Partner Agreement (proposed)

Term: 12 mėn., auto-renew

Vet clinic gives:
- Promotion materijalas Longrein'ui vet'o waiting room'e (paper handout)
- Mention'inimas Longrein per žirgo savininkų educational sesijas
- Permission naudoti vet'o logo ant longrein.eu/partners
- 1 testimonial / case study per metus (from a stable they introduced)

Longrein gives:
- "Trusted Vet Partner" badge for vet clinic to use
- 50% discount Longrein vet'o pačiam (jei jis turi savo stable)
- Free integration setup — vet sistema → Longrein health records (one-way sync)
- 10% revenue share for first 12 mėn. of vet-attributed customer'ių

Mutual:
- Joint webinar 1× per metus apie horse welfare protocols
- Quarterly check-in
```

### Estimated economics

- Per top regional vet clinic: 50–150 stable touchpoints per metai
- Penetration target Year 1: 3% → 2–4 customers per vet
- 5 top vets × 3 customers/yr = 15 customer'ių per metai per vet channel
- 10% revenue share = small cost (~€100/customer/yr)

### Outreach email template (Vet Clinic — professional, peer-to-peer)

**Subject:** Žirgo sveikatos record'ai — automatizacijos partnership su Longrein

```
Sveiki [Vet Clinic Director vardas],

Esu Andreja Daranda — Trakų Jojimo Klubas savininkė ir Longrein founder. Longrein'as yra stable management software'a, kurią mes patys naudojame Trakuose, ir kurią dabar atveriame Lietuvos žirgynams.

Vienas iš stipriausių use case'ų yra žirgo sveikatos tracking'as — vakcinacijos, farrier, vet visit history. Ir tai yra vieta, kur jūsų klinika ir mūsų software galėtume sukurti rimtą value flow:

Šiandien — vet aplankai stable'ą, įrašei into vet sistemą, stable savininkas tipiškai iš naujo įveda informaciją į savo Excel arba popierinį dienoraštį (jei iš viso). Duplications + clinical data atsijungimas.

Su Longrein integration'a — vet sistema vienkartiškai rašo health record'ą ir tas record'as automatiškai pasirodo stable'es Longrein health timeline. Pacijentų istorija visada švari ir prieinama.

Pasiūlymas:
- Mes integruosim su jūsų sistema (free)
- Jūs paminėsit Longrein'ą savo klientams (paper handout, education sessions)
- 10% revenue share customer'iams, kuriuos atvyksta per jūsų rekomendaciją
- Co-branded webinar apie preventive care + welfare tracking

Ar galite skirti 30 min Zoom susitikimui? Galim ir asmeniškai jei tinkam'a (esu Trakuose).

Andreja Daranda
andreja@longrein.eu
```

### KPIs vet partnership'ui

- Customer acquisitions per vet partner per quarter (target: 1–3)
- Health record integration uptake by Longrein customers (target: 50%+ of stable customers using vet integration by M18)
- Joint webinar attendance (target: 30+ stable owners per webinar)

## Dalis 4 — Partner Type #3: Feed Suppliers

### Kontekstas

Feed/supplement distributors LT/PL serve EVERY stable. They have:
- Direct mailing lists of stable owners
- Trade show booths at equestrian events
- Frequent (monthly) touchpoint via deliveries
- Brand authority (premium feed brands — Cavalor, St. Hippolyt, Pavo)

### What we want from feed suppliers

1. **Inclusion in their marketing collateral** (catalogs, newsletter)
2. **Trade show co-presence** (their booth, our roll-up banner)
3. **Customer mailing access** (1–2 email's per year promoting Longrein)
4. **Cross-sell at point of sale** — when stable orders €500+ feed, supplier sends Longrein info pack

### What feed suppliers want

Feed companies are different — they don't typically do revenue share. They want:
- **Brand association** (Longrein's premium positioning aligns with premium feed brands)
- **Customer engagement content** (case studies showing how stables track feed costs in Longrein)
- **Event co-marketing** (joint trade show, joint educational content)

### Partnership model siūlymas

```
Feed Supplier Co-Marketing Agreement (proposed)

NE revenue share. NE referral fee. PURE co-marketing.

Term: 12 mėn.

Feed supplier gives:
- 2 email mentions per year to their LT/PL customer list
- 1 trade show co-presence per year
- Catalog mention (text + Longrein logo) in annual product catalog
- Sales rep mention at point-of-sale visits

Longrein gives:
- "Recommended by [feed brand]" badge for landing page
- 1 free Longrein account for feed supplier's own use (internal trial)
- Per-horse feed cost tracker (built into Longrein product, attributable to feed supplier)
- Joint thought leadership content (1 article / year)

Mutual:
- Performance review at 6 + 12 mo
```

### Partnership model

## Dalis 8 — Common channel partnership mistakes (kurių NEdaryti)

1. **„Mes pasiūlykim 50% revenue share!"** — Partner pajunta, kad esi desperate'as. Plus customer LTV'as padalintas pusiau visada.
2. **Pirmas single big partnership.** Diversifikuok — jei vienas partner'is dingsta, channel'as nepakirstas.
3. **Co-marketing be measurement'o.** Kiekvienas partneris turi atributable signup link/code, kad žinotum, kuris veikia.
4. **Sutik partnership'ą prieš onboarding'ą.** Kai LSF email blast'as veikia ir 50 leads atskrenda — kas juos onboardins? Direct sales bandwidth'as pirma, channel antra.
5. **Niekada nepasakyk „my product is too small for that partner".** Federation'ams reikia digital partner'ių. Vet'ams reikia data integration'os. Tu atneši vertę, kuri jiems nematoma.
6. **Niekada nesusukit on price'ą su partner'ių „discount expectation".** Jei partner'is sako „mūsų member'iai tikisi 30% off" — tu sakai „mes pasiūlom 15%, ir tai yra vertė pakankama. Jei ne, mūsų partnership'as ne fit."
7. **NEpasiekti pirmo partnership'o pirmus 6 mėn.** Direct customers proves PMF. Channel'as multiplies PMF'ą — bet jei nera ką multiply, channel'as fail'ina.
