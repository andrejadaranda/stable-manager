## Description (4000 chars)

> Longrein is the operational backbone for serious European riding stables.
>
> Built for owners, trainers, clients, and horse owners — Longrein replaces the WhatsApp groups, paper diaries, and Excel sheets that hold most stables together. Everything from booking the next lesson to logging this morning's farrier visit lives in one premium, EU-hosted app.
>
> WHAT YOU GET
>
> • Live GPS ride tracker — Strava-style route map, elevation, pace, gait breakdown (walk/trot/canter/gallop minutes), per-km splits, calorie estimate. Share a branded post-ride card straight to Instagram.
>
> • Live safety beacon — share a public read-only link with your emergency contact before you ride. They see your live position; if you stop transmitting for 10 minutes the page shows a red alarm.
>
> • Horse management — full profile per horse: photos, bio, lineage (sire/dam), microchip, passport, FEI ID, height, discipline. Welfare workload scoring keeps every horse in the right weekly load band.
>
> • Multi-party health log — invite your vet or farrier via a magic-link and let them post visits directly into the horse's health log. No account needed. Owner gets a reminder 7, 3, and 1 day before the next vaccination, shoeing, or vet visit is due.
>
> • Lessons & calendar — book, drag-to-reschedule, recurring weekly lessons (4–12 weeks in one click), 24-hour email reminders.
>
> • Payments & finance — track cash, card, and transfer payments. Monthly P&L. Prepaid lesson packages. Boarding charges generated in one click. Stripe Checkout built in.
>
> • Multi-role app — owner, trainer, client, horse owner. Chat any-to-any within your stable. Lesson requests + care requests in one inbox.
>
> • Weather alerts — get an email when tomorrow's forecast crosses a freeze or heat threshold you set. Helps grooms plan blankets, fly masks, and ride windows.
>
> • Your brand, not ours — upload your stable logo + brand color. Emails, share cards, and the app header all use YOUR identity. Riders see your stable first.
>
> EU-FIRST, GDPR-NATIVE
>
> Hosted in Frankfurt. Pan-European product — Lithuanian, German, French, and Polish keywords supported for SEO. Stripe Checkout LIVE. Row-level security per stable. 14-day free trial — card required, cancel any time before day 14 and you're not charged.
>
> WHO IT'S FOR
>
> • Riding clubs (5–50 horses) replacing paper or Excel
> • Trainers running freelance lessons across multiple yards
> • Stable owners who want premium client experience without paying €200/mo for outdated software
>
> Founding 15 spots at €25/month locked-for-life — apply at longrein.eu
