## Personalisation checklist (every email, every time)

Before sending, every email needs:

- [ ] First name spelled correctly (check Instagram, website, federation list)
- [ ] One specific signal you noticed about their stable — Instagram post, website detail, federation listing, mutual acquaintance
- [ ] No generic adjectives ("amazing", "incredible", "world-class", "comprehensive")
- [ ] Reading time: under 90 seconds. If longer, cut.
- [ ] No attachments — link to longrein.eu, not a PDF
- [ ] Subject under 60 characters
- [ ] Sent from `hello@longrein.eu` (not personal Gmail)
- [ ] Sent Tuesday–Thursday between 10:00 and 14:00 their local time
