# Landing Page Copy — Top 2 Brand Names

Two complete landing-page drafts, ready to hand to a designer or paste straight into a Next.js page. Each has the same structure so you can A/B-test the brand alongside the messaging.

The hero block is the most important 200 pixels of your business — that's the section I've put the most weight into.

## 1. Hayloft

### Brand voice
Warm, archive-feeling, premium-rural. Hayloft is the home where every horse's story lives — visible to the people who care about that horse. Trust, transparency, quiet confidence. Never cute. The visual brand should feel like warm wood, soft daylight, and a leather-bound logbook — not a tech startup.

### Hero
- **Eyebrow:** For modern riding stables
- **Headline:** **Every horse. Every ride. In one place.**
- **Subhead:** Hayloft is the operating system for stables that take riding seriously. Trainers log sessions in 15 seconds. Owners and riders see exactly how their horse is doing — without a single message in WhatsApp.
- **Primary CTA:** Start free for 14 days
- **Secondary CTA:** Watch a 90-second demo
- **Hero visual:** A trainer's phone showing the session-log screen — three taps, one submit. Background: a warm-lit barn aisle, slightly defocused. No stock photos. Hire a real photographer for a half-day at a real stable.

### Trust bar (placeholder for first 5 customers)
*"Loved by stables in 4 countries — Germany, Austria, Netherlands, Poland."*

### Three-feature block

**Log a session in 15 seconds.**
A trainer's tool, built one-handed. Tap the horse. Tap the type. Tap submit. Move on with your day. Every session lands on the horse's profile, the rider's feed, and the owner's view — automatically.

**Owners see what they pay for.**
Every owner gets a free Hayloft account showing their horse's full activity. No more "did anyone ride Apollo today?" texts at 9pm. Transparency builds trust. Trust keeps boarders.

**The dashboard your stable already needs.**
Lessons today. Activity heatmap for every horse. Under-worked horses flagged. The view your head trainer keeps open on the office iPad. Replaces three apps and a notebook.

### How it works (3 steps with screenshots)
1. **Add your horses.** Three minutes, three fields each. We'll seed a few demo horses so you can play first.
2. **Invite your trainers and clients.** Email-only, no setup. Clients are free, forever.
3. **Log your first session.** Hayloft does the rest — history, dashboards, notifications.

### Pricing
- **Starter — €39/mo.** Up to 10 horses, 2 staff seats. For small training stables.
- **Pro — €79/mo.** Up to 30 horses, 6 staff seats. The plan most stables choose.
- **Premium — €149/mo.** Unlimited horses, multi-location, priority support.

*14-day free trial. No credit card.*

**Run your stable in 10 minutes a day.**
Start your free trial. Cancel anytime — though most don't.

## 2. Cadence

### Brand voice
Crisp, modern, performance-feeling. Cadence is the rhythm your stable runs on. Built for trainers who care about results and clients who care about progress. Confidence over warmth, premium without being cold. The visual brand should feel like dressage white-and-navy: clean lines, decisive type, restrained palette, one accent.

### Hero
- **Eyebrow:** For training stables that take performance seriously
- **Headline:** **The rhythm your stable runs on.**
- **Subhead:** Cadence turns every ride into structured data. Trainers log in seconds. Riders track real progress. Owners see every session. The first stable platform that actually moves the needle.
- **Primary CTA:** Start your 14-day trial
- **Secondary CTA:** See how it works
- **Hero visual:** A clean dashboard view — horse-activity heatmap, this-week's-sessions count, with a phone overlay showing the log-session screen. Crisp white background, navy + accent colour. Show the *product*, not a horse photo.

### Trust bar
*"Trusted by 50+ training stables across Europe."*

### Three-feature block

**Built for the way you actually log.**
Sessions captured in 15 seconds, one-handed, in the barn. Auto-fill from your last entry. Repeat with one tap. Cadence is the only stable platform that respects how trainers actually work.

**Real progress, finally measurable.**
Every session feeds your horse's activity history and your rider's training journal. See workload at a glance. Catch under-trained horses before owners do. Reward your best clients with measurable progress.

**One source of truth across your stable.**
Owners. Riders. Trainers. Yourself. Everyone sees the right view of the same data. No more screenshots, WhatsApp threads, or "did anyone ride today?" Cadence ends that conversation.

### How it works
1. **Set up your stable in 3 minutes.** Pre-loaded with demo data so you can explore first.
2. **Invite your team and clients.** Free for clients. Forever.
3. **Log your first session.** Start the rhythm.

## Pre-launch checklist (whichever name you pick)

1. Buy the .com (or strongest country TLD) the day you decide.
2. Lock the IG handle and TikTok handle within the same hour.
3. Trademark search — EUIPO + USPTO, classes 9 and 42.
4. Hire a half-day with a real photographer at a real stable. The hero photo is the single biggest visual asset you'll own.
5. Build a one-page site with hero + features + pricing + email capture. **Deploy before you have product.** Drive your first 30 IG conversations through it.
6. Add Plausible + PostHog from day one. Every visitor matters when you have 50 of them.
